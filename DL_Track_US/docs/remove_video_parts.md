- Once you started the GUI and the main GUI window opened, click on the
<span style="color: #a34ba1;">**Advanced Methods**</span>.
- In the <span style="color: #2eaf66;">**Select Method**</span> Dropdown select “Crop Video”. The separate
“Crop Video Window” will pop up.

<img src="\md_graphics\remove_video_parts\advanced_methods.png">
<img src="\md_graphics\remove_video_parts\select_method.png" width ="120">
<img src="\md_graphics\remove_video_parts\remove_video_parts_window.png" width="450">

- First, you need to specify the video you want to remove parts from. Click the <span style="color: #a34ba1;">**Load Video**</span> button and select the video.

<img src="\md_graphics\remove_video_parts\load_video_button.png" width="450">

After successfully loading the video, the UI should look like this.

- With the yellow slider you can watch through the video.
- By clicking and dragging on the image with the left mouse button you can select the part you want to remove.

<img src="\md_graphics\remove_video_parts\loaded_video.png" width="600">

- By clicking the <span style="color: #a34ba1;">**Browse**</span> button you can selcect the folder, where the cropped video is saved.
- Finally, click <span style="color: #2eaf66;">**Remove Parts**</span>.

<img src="\md_graphics\remove_video_parts\output_path.png" width="600">
